ABOUT US






Mental Make-Up facilitates opportunities for persons with chronic mental illness to reach their optimal level of independent functioning in the society and for improving their quality of life.


MM also embraces spiritual practices that can help to reduce stress and restore one's well-being. This company's center includes yoga, light physical exercises, group discussion, social skills, individual counselling and more.









VISION

"To empower people to achieve their highest level of recovery and participation in life through excellence."

MISSION

"Mental Make-up aims at improving emotional, social, and intellectual skilld needed to live, learn and work in the community with least amount of dependency."

VALUES

"Excellence in care, demonstrated through: innovation, integrity, service, compassion, teamwork and continuous learning."
